Highest Security and Enhanced Productivity

Is your organization equipped with the tools to empower your employees, no matter where they’re working? In a situation where employees may be unable to report to the office, it’s essential that they still have secure access to the data and apps they need, whether they use corporate or personal owned devices.

Give your employees the flexibility to work using any personal device. BlackBerry Spark® UEM Suites offer a single platform that streamlines the management process across devices, apps, files and users.

BlackBerry Spark UEM Suites can be delivered on premises, as a cloud service or in a mixed model. Whichever deployment you choose, you’ll get reliable BlackBerry® technical support, disaster recovery features and mobility uptime you can count on. As with all the BlackBerry Spark Suites, the subscription model eliminates upfront capital costs and allows predictable yearly operating expenses.

Solution Brief Evaluation Guide

Mobile Device Management (MDM)

BlackBerry Spark UEM Suites deliver consistent MDM policies and controls across key platforms (iOS®, Android™, Android™ for Enterprise, Windows®, macOS®, BlackBerry®, Linux® and Samsung Knox™) and device ownership models.

Mobile Application Management (MAM)

Mobilize your critical workflows, business processes and application, including Microsoft® Office mobile apps. BlackBerry provides a seamless connection and management between our secure mobile container and your Microsoft Office mobile apps.

Mobile Content Management (MCM)

Access your business files from Microsoft® SharePoint®, Microsoft OneDrive®, Box™ and more, all with native document editing capabilities. Secure files even outside the firewall with BlackBerry® Workspaces, a leading secure EFSS solution. With the BlackBerry UEM Suite, you get BlackBerry® Workspaces Secure Plus Edition to enable your organization to establish view, edit, print and share permissions, set content expiry dates, or revoke access to files.

Identity & Access Management (IAM)

The BlackBerry Spark UEM Suite includes Identity & Access Management (IAM) with Microsoft® Active Directory® integration, Kerberos, OIDC and SAML support for browser access and two-factor authentication options for secure apps.

  • BlackBerry® Enterprise Identity – Single sign-on for enterprise applications
  • BlackBerry® 2FA – two-factor authentication for remote file access to support a Zero Touch strategy

Advanced Productivity Apps and Capabilities Built-in

The BlackBerry Spark UEM Suite includes enhanced collaboration apps and capabilities for an optimized user experience on both iOS® an Android™:

  • BlackBerry® Notes – a secure note-taking solution designed for enterprise users.
  • BlackBerry® Edit – an all-in-one enterprise document solution that streamlines mobile editing.

SDKs for Developing & Deploying Secure Apps

The BlackBerry Spark UEM Suite offers software development kits (API/SDKs) for a wide range of app development tools, so you can incorporate BlackBerry® security into your custom mobile app. Ensure consistent protection across devices and key platforms -- including encryption, secure communications, DLP policies and more.
